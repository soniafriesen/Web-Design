Who's Queen?

Queen is a British rock band from the 1970’s to the early 90’s and is considered one of the greatest rock bands of all time. Back when Queen first started, it was known as Smile. A college band made up of Roger Taylor(drummer), Brian May (lead guitarist), and Tim Staffell (vocalist).Tim Staffell left the band allowing Freddie Mercury to be the new front man of Smile. They changed the name to Queen when Freddie Mercury joined. Following Freddie Mercury was John Deacon (bass guitarist). The logo in Queen is made up of the band's different zodiac symbols. Two lions for leo which are John Deacon and Roger Taylor, a crab for Cancer which is Brian May, and two fairies for Virgo which was Freddie Mercury. Today, Queen is still well known amoung generations and will be for generations to come. They made fantastic music shaping todays pop culture.
